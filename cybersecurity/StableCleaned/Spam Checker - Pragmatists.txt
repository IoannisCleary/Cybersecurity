<p>There various online tools that check the deliverability of a message by assigning a quality score. This score determines how likely it is to be marked as spam based on the subject, content and the sender of the message.</p>

<p>ISnotSpam and Mail-Tester helps determine if a message will be classified as spam by forwarding the email to a given address and viewing the report</p>

<p><strong>Spam detection</strong></p>

<p>In order to activate spam detection you can use a generic test string called GTUBE that is used to test/trigger spam filters.</p>

<p>XJS*C4JDBQADN1.NSBN3*2IDNEN*GTUBE-STANDARD-ANTI-UBE-TEST-EMAIL*C.34X</p>
